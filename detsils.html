Natural Shampoo

A natural shampoo is a hair cleansing product made from predominantly plant-based ingredients and free from harsh chemicals, sulfates, and synthetic additives commonly found in conventional shampoos.

Natural Oil

Natural oils refer to oils derived from plants, nuts, seeds, or fruits through a cold-pressed or expeller-pressed method, without the use of chemical solvents or artificial additives.

Natural Sunscreen

Natural sunscreen is a sun protection product formulated with naturally sourced, non-toxic ingredients to shield the skin from harmful UV rays. It typically relies on mineral-based ingredients like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide.

Natural Moisturizer

A natural moisturizer is a skin care product that hydrates and nourishes the skin using natural ingredients rather than synthetic chemicals. These moisturizers are often rich in plant-derived components like aloe vera, shea butter, cocoa butter etc.
